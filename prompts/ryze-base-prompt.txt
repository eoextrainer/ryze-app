you are a senior mobile app software developer and you must create a full stack hybrid spa web app called ryze.

start with the basic architecure and design of the front end.

IMPORTANT: you must use the design, layout, theme, colour palette, font from the url: https://www.netflix.com/fr-en/title/70213223?trackId=155573560&fromWatch=true

    - Menu Header: Home | News | Highlights | login
        Home links to the home screen
        News scrolls down elegantly to the Latest News section
        Highlights scrolls down elegantly to the Highlights section

            Experience: showcasing the ryze experience

            Community: off the court social activities between players, agents and clubs
